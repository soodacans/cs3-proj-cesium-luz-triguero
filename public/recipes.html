I. Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

150 g unsalted butter,
100 g light or dark brown sugar
60 g granulated sugar
1 large egg
½ tsp vanilla bean paste or vanilla extract
150 g all-purpose flour
¼ tsp baking powder
¼ tsp baking soda
3 g (1 tsp) salt

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Preheat the oven to 350°f / 180°c. Line two sheet pans with parchment paper
2. Place the butter in a medium saucepan, and place over medium heat. Cook until the butter has melted, and then continue to cook. Swirl the pan and stir with a whisk, until the butter foams and turns golden brown. This should take 3-4 minutes.
3. Weigh out 110g of the brown butter into a medium bowl and leave to cool for 15-20 minutes.
4. Add the brown sugar and granulated sugar to the bowl with the brown butter, and mix to combine.
5. Add the egg and whisk well for 1-2 minutes until the mixture has lightened in colour and has thickened.
6. Add the vanilla and mix well.
7. Add the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix to combine until there are some flour streaks remaining, to ensure you do not over work the dough.
8. Add the chocolate and mix to incorporate with a rubber spatula.
9. Scoop 6 cookies (about 55g per cookie dough ball) using a 2 Tbsp cookie scoop onto the prepared baking sheet. Arrange evenly apart on the pan. Keep the remainder of the batter in the bowl lightly covered with plastic wrap.
10. Bake the cookies for 11-13 minutes. Bake for longer if you would like your cookies to be crispier. Remove from the oven and bang the pan briefly on the counter to help deflate the cookies.

II. S'mores Ice Cream Sundae

Ingredients:

For the chocolate sauce:
3 ½ ounces (100g) semisweet chocolate
¼ cup (30g) confectioners' sugar
⅓ cup (100g) light corn syrup
⅓ cup (80ml) heavy cream
Pinch of salt
4 graham crackers
2 cups (4 scoops) vanilla or toasted marshmallow ice cream
½ quiantity vanilla marshmallow frosting
1 handful of marshmallows

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Break the chocolate in crushed pieces
2. Melt it in a heatproof bowl set over a saucepan of barely simmering water. Once melted, stir in the sugar, syrup, cream, and salt
3. Crumble two crackers into the bases of four sundae glasses or bowls.
4. Scoop in half the ice cream on top of the crumbs. Pipe or poon in half the marshmallow frosting, then drizzle with chocolate sauce.
5. Drizzle with more chocolate suace, then add more cracker crumbs on the top.
6. Position the remaining marshmallows on top of each sundae. You may use a kitchen torch to scorch the marshmallows to perfection.

III. Crinkles

Ingredients:

⅓ cup vegetable oil
½ cup brown sugar
¼ cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
½ tsp salt
⅓ cup cocoa
1 cup AP flour
¼ tsp baking soda
powdered sugar

Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. Mix the oil, brown sugar, white sugar, egg, and vanilla.
2. Sift in the flour, salt, cocoa powder, and baking soda.
3. Combine well.
4. Scoop around 2 tablespoons of dough and roll in a bowl of white sugar.
5. Then, take the ball of dough and roll it in powdered sugar.
6. Bake for 12-13 minutes and cool for 10 minutes.
